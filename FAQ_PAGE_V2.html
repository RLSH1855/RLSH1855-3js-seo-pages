Spray-In Bedliners

5 questions

A spray-in bedliner is a protective coating sprayed directly onto your truck bed, creating a permanent, seamless barrier against dents, scratches, rust, and UV damage. Unlike drop-in liners, it won't shift, crack, or trap moisture — it bonds to the metal and stays there for life.

🛡️ Permanent Protection

A spray-in bedliner bonds permanently to your truck bed, is custom-fit to every contour, and cannot trap water or debris underneath. A drop-in liner sits loosely in the bed, can shift during use, and allows moisture to collect underneath — which can cause rust over time. Spray-in is the superior, longer-lasting option.

⚖️ Spray-In vs. Drop-In

A professionally applied spray-in bedliner from 3J's Auto Body is permanent and comes with a lifetime warranty. With normal use it will last the life of your truck — no cracking, peeling, or fading.

🛡️ Lifetime Warranty
